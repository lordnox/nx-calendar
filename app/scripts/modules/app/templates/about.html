Node.js has quickly become my weapon of choice for most of the work I am doing. It's fast to setup a custom server. Horizontal scalabilty is fantastic and npm is one of the most powerful package managers I have seen so far.

Yeoman

Yeoman is your helper to create webapps in an instant. It will install all things you need to develop the product you choose and not have to worry about any boilerplate

bower

Bower

Bower is for the browser what npm is for node. It handles your package needs. When you need to format times and dates. Just use bower install moment and a brilliant date and time library is installed in your working directory for you to include in your layout.

Grunt

Grunt is probably the most important tool at your disposal. Learn it! Through the commandline tool grunt and a little Gruntfile.js your projects starts breathing. It handles everything from compiling your assets (coffeescript, sass, etc.)
